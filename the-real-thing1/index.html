Our Story

The Indie Food website was launched by four CFGers who wanted to create a website to find and share the best independent restaurants in London.



Who we are

We are a group of food lovers dedicated to finding the best restaurants in London

Our mission

As big brands are continually dominating the food industry, we believe that it is important to support our local restaurants and cafés. On this website you will be able to discover a variety of niché places all over London!

Contact us

And why not get involved? You can head to our contact form page and share your favourite independent restaurants with us!
